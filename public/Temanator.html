Teamanator

May 2020 - August 2020

What the client needed

My team and I were looking to develop an app that would give a user flexibility to manage teams beyond the typical HR applications. Our goal was to use react.js and mongo.db to display all the elements that a user needs to manage a team, including a calendar and dashboard.

My part of the project

I assisted my team with management of the project overall. I am a strong believer that organization is what makes or breaks a project. I also designed the logo and built the homepage of the application.

Additional Project Info
