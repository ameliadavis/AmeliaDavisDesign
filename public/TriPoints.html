TriPoints

March 2020 - April 2020

What the client needed

Most people who purchase a new car don't take into account all the additional costs associated with owning a car. This app was designed and built to assist with these decisions on mercedes -benz.

My part of the project

My team and I utilized a free API for gas prices based on state and built an API for the Mercedes maintenance information. I designed the logo and front end user-interface for the site while my team built the backend and interactions with the data. We used the UI-kit CSS library, as well as mysql, and sequelize to interact with the database.
